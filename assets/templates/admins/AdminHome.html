About the System

Lumpy Skin Disease Detection Platform

This system uses deep learning techniques — VGG-16, VGG-19, and Inception-v3 — to detect Lumpy Skin Disease in cattle by analysing skin images uploaded by registered users. Early identification of the disease reduces economic losses and supports better livestock health management across affected regions.
